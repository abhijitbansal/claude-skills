Known deferred limitations — documented, not bugs to test
  • A manual resume during a pending auto-resume may yield one extra harmless continue at reset.
  • Two watchers/configs on one machine share one ~/.wind/state.json (single-watcher-per-machine is the supported mode).
  • A stale limit message lingering past the resume cooldown could re-pause a session (rare; agent idle >10 min).

If something fails

  • Note the exact card/command, expected vs actual, and any devtools console error.
  • Restart wind dash after refreshing ~/.wind/dashboard.html via install.sh.
  • A GATE failure blocks merge.
  • A REG failure on an XSS/token check means a security guarantee is broken — stop and report before merging.
